Universal Metal Supply Ltd. (UMS) was established in 2014. We are an Alberta based metal supplier & aluminum manufacturer, located in Calgary, specializing in aluminum extrusions, aluminum railings, fences, gates, and any custom-made metal products for our customers.

Our goal is to provide superior service and quality products to wide variety of customers across Alberta.

Our concentration is to provide a wide selection of aluminum products(either raw materials or finished), quickly and easily, with no minimum order size, and fast shipping. Call ahead, order online or visit our warehouse.

There's no need to buy more than you need, and store your inventory. You simply purchase the exact right quantities you want.

Monday to Friday: 8:30 AM - 4:00 PM
Saturday: by appointment

We welcome our customers to stop in and visit us anytime.
